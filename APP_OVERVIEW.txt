==========================================

CORE COMPONENTS & FEATURES
--------------------------

1. GREETING & PROFILE:
   Displays a personalized greeting ("Good Morning, User!") based on the AuthContext.

2. PROMO BANNER:
   A dynamic, animated carousel showing current offers, discounts, and health tips.

3. QUICK ACTIONS:
   - "Upload Prescription": Direct entry to the prescription digitization flow.
   - "Medicines": Browse the full pharmacy inventory.
   - "Doctor Chat": Instant connection to the Health Concierge.

4. REAL-TIME ACTIVITY:
   Displays the latest status of the user's active prescriptions and orders directly on the home screen.

5. THEME SUPPORT:
   Full integration with system appearance (Light/Dark mode) using a curated premium color palette.
